Preplacement training

Pre-Placement Training (PPT) is a crucial part of the university's Placement Support Program, designed to prepare students for the competitive job market. The training typically takes place before the placement season begins and aims to equip students with the skills necessary to secure their desired job offers. Pre-Placement Training ensures that students are not only academically qualified but also prepared in terms of personal development, interview techniques, and industry knowledge, giving them the best possible chance for success during the placement process. our university mainly focuses on the company requirements and trains the students according to it.During the journey the students gains the confidence and ability to attend the interviews and made their life colourful.
