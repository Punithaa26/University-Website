Academic Programs

Arcadia University offers a wide range of academic programs and majors. Some of the popular majors include:

  • Bachelor's Degrees: Accounting, Biology, Business Administration, Computer Science, Criminology and Criminal Justice, and more.
  • Graduate Programs: Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Education (MEd), Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT), and more.
  • Doctorate Programs: Research and scholarship doctorates.

School of Computing

The School of Computing at Arcadia University offers a variety of programs designed to provide students with a strong foundation in computing principles and practices. A school of computing is a higher education institution that offers programs and courses related to computing and computer science. Schools of computing offer programs at the undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD levels. Some programs include Bachelor of Computer Applications, B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering, Master of Computer Application, and Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Science and Engineering.

School of Engineering

Arcadia School of Engineering is committed to research, undertaking projects funded by various agencies including DST, INUP, ISRO RESPOND, and UGC. A school of engineering is an institution that offers education in science and technology, and prepares students to become engineers. Some schools of engineering focus on research, employability, and lifelong learning skills. Others focus on developing scientific temper and preparing engineers for industry and scientific organizations.

School of Commerce and Management

The School of Commerce and Management stands as an inspiration for academic excellence, cultivating future leaders in the dynamic field of commerce and management. Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) in Management Studies is a 3-year undergraduate Management program which deals with the study of organizational behaviour, how organizations work, learning to explore the principles of managing people in organizations, and developing a modern view through examining a part of the context.

School of Pharmaceutical Sciences

Arcadia School of Pharmaceutical Sciences provides excellent opportunities for aspiring pharmacists in both pharmaceutical industries and healthcare multinational corporations in India. Offering a comprehensive range of programs, including B.Pharm, PharmD, PharmD (Post Baccalaureate), M.Pharm (Pharmaceutics, Pharmaceutical Analysis), MB School of Pharmaceutical Sciences provides excellent opportunities for aspiring pharmacists in both pharmaceutical industries and healthcare multinational corporations in India. Recognized as one of the leading institutions in the country, the school offers an outstanding curriculum designed to equip students with professional capabilities.

School of Agriculture

School of Agriculture aims to bring new paradigms in learning various sciences and technologies embraced in the field of agriculture and allied fields like Horticulture, Agribusiness Management, Animal Husbandry, Food Science. We wish to create its own niche by bringing together the industry and service sectors related to agriculture/farming with a unique curriculum & knowledge-sharing platforms that enhance the skills and employability of the students. We primarily focus on encouraging students in the development of business acumen through the proper nurturing of ideas for the development of business proposals, execution & implementation.
