Our History

Founded in 1965, Arcadia University has a proud legacy of academic excellence and community involvement. Over the years, we have built a reputation for providing a top-tier education while nurturing personal and intellectual growth. From our humble beginnings as a small liberal arts college to our current status as a world-renowned institution, Arcadia University has been a beacon of knowledge, diversity, and innovation. In addition to our strong academic programs, Arcadia University offers a vibrant campus life filled with opportunities for students to explore their passions and develop lifelong friendships. Our beautiful campus, located just outside of Philadelphia, provides a perfect setting for learning and growth. Students can participate in a wide range of extracurricular activities, including clubs, sports, and volunteer work. Our dedicated faculty members are committed to providing students with a personalized education that prepares them for success in the 21st century. With a focus on critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills, our curriculum empowers students to become well-rounded individuals who are ready to make a difference in the world. Arcadia University is also committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive community. We believe that diversity enriches the learning experience and prepares students to thrive in a globalized world. Our diverse student body hails from all over the world, bringing a wealth of perspectives and experiences to the classroom. Whether you're interested in pursuing a bachelor's, master's, or doctoral degree, Arcadia University has a program to meet your needs. Our graduate programs offer advanced training in a variety of fields, including business, education, psychology, and more.
